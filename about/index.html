About me

AI engineer, researcher, and product builder.

Who I am

I am an AI and data engineer based in Zurich and a PhD candidate in Artificial Intelligence at the Universidad Politécnica de Madrid. My research is supervised by Dr. Daniel Garijo in the Ontology Engineering Group.

What I work on

I build systems that turn complex or unstructured information into data people and software can use. My work includes knowledge graphs, ontology engineering, research software classification, document processing, machine learning, and data platforms.

Research and practical systems

My research asks how software and scientific knowledge can be classified, aligned, and reused. My engineering work tests those ideas against production constraints: imperfect data, reliability, maintainability, and the needs of the people using the system.

What I am interested in now

I am currently focused on intelligent knowledge systems, research software, document AI, semantic technologies, and products that combine structured knowledge with useful interfaces.
