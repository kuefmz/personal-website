Congratulations to my friend Ágoston Sipos on successfully defending his PhD thesis, *Implicit Surface Patches in 3D Geometric Modeling*.

I have known Ágoston since our Bolyai college days. Over the years, our friendship has included long conversations through the pandemic, a memorable bicycle trip around Lake Balaton, and a late-night explanation of Turing machines before I had to teach the topic the following day.

Academic careers matter, but the people who help us learn and keep going matter even more. I am proud of what he achieved and grateful for the friendship behind all those shared moments.
