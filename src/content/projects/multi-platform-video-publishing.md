This is a product idea, not a released automation solution. The goal is to reduce the repetitive work involved in adapting and publishing video content across multiple platforms.

## Proposed workflow

The service would accept a source video, prepare platform-specific formats and metadata, schedule publication, and report failures or required manual review. AI could assist with bounded tasks such as draft captions, but the core value is dependable orchestration through official platform APIs.

The next step is product discovery: validate creator needs, confirm API constraints, define privacy expectations, and build a narrow prototype before presenting it as a working product.
