The Sex Toy Ontology models products, materials, anatomy, use cases, safety properties, and compatibility in a domain with inconsistent terminology. It powers the public KISS AI demo.

The project applies ontology-engineering methods to product search and information quality while accounting for inclusivity, privacy, and safety. The semantic model is intended to support clearer discovery, catalog interoperability, and explainable recommendations.
