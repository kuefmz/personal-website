The Sex Toy Ontology explores the representation of products, materials, anatomy, use cases, safety properties, and compatibility in a domain where terminology is often inconsistent or incomplete. It now powers a public KISS AI demo.

The project applies ontology-engineering methods to a product space with real challenges around inclusivity, privacy, safety, and search.

## From vocabulary to product infrastructure

A well-designed semantic model can support better discovery, explainable recommendations, catalog interoperability, and safer product information. The live demo turns that semantic foundation into an accessible product experience while the underlying model continues to evolve.
