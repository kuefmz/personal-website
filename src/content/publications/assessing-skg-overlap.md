Scientific knowledge graphs use different taxonomies to describe research outputs. This makes their overlaps, gaps, and disagreements difficult to understand.

The paper introduces a bottom-up methodology based on category annotations attached to instances shared between graphs. Applied to 100,000 publications in OpenAlex and OpenAIRE, the method produced an alignment of 71 categories and quantified agreement between the two systems.
