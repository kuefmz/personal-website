This master's thesis developed a way to classify research software repositories by function and scientific domain using their documentation.

The method was designed to accommodate new categories without retraining a traditional fixed-label classifier. It achieved a 92% F1 score during training and 76% on the test set, compared with a 36% prior baseline, and was integrated into the SOMEF metadata extraction framework.
